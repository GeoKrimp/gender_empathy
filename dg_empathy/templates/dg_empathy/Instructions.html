Instructions

This is the second part of the task. You are now paired with another participant. Your earnings in this part depend only on the decision you make.

You have been provisionally allocated 20 cents, while the other participant has been allocated nothing. You must decide how much, if any, of your 20 cents you would like to transfer to the other participant. You may transfer any amount between 0 and 20 cents.

The other participant has no decision to make, is a real person, and will receive exactly the amount you choose to transfer. No deception is used in this study. The amount you decide to keep will be added to the payment you have already earned for completing the survey.
